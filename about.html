The Mechanic Who Comes to You

Kent Services is not a call center or a lead-matching app. It is a real local shop on wheels, run by someone who lives and works right here in Central Florida.

Meet Jacob

Kent Services is owned and operated by Jacob Kent, a Leesburg-based mechanic who got tired of watching people get stuck with long shop waits, surprise fees and the hassle of arranging a tow just to get a vehicle looked at. So he brought the shop to the customer instead.

Today, Jacob covers Lake, Sumter and Marion counties, showing up to driveways, workplaces, farms, jobsites and the side of the road. Cars and trucks are the bread and butter, but he also keeps tractors, skid steers, generators, forklifts, mowers and golf carts running, and offers mobile welding on top of it. That range is rare, and it is why a lot of property and equipment owners keep his number saved.

Text-First, On Purpose

Jacob prefers a text over a call, and there is a good reason for it. A text lets him see exactly what you need, keep the details straight, and get back to you without anything slipping through the cracks on a busy day. So if you want the fastest response, send a text with your location and what is going on. Photos help.

Straight Answers, Fair Prices

The approach is simple: show up, find the real problem, and give you an honest price before any work starts. We will never talk you into work you do not need. If something is not worth fixing, we will tell you straight rather than sink your money into a repair that is going to fail again shortly. The flat $125 call-out and diagnostic fee covers everything you have on site, and it comes off your labor total if you move forward with the repair.
