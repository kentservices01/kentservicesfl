Mobile Auto Diagnostics & Electrical Repair

A warning light, a no-start or a strange electrical gremlin does not have to mean a tow. We bring professional-grade diagnostic tools to your driveway or the roadside, pinpoint the real problem, and give you a straight answer on what it takes to fix it.

Find the Real Problem, Not a Guess

Modern vehicles throw dozens of trouble codes, and a single warning light can point to several different issues. We plug in, read the live data, and test the actual components instead of throwing parts at the problem. That means you pay to fix what is wrong, not what might be wrong.

Our diagnostic and call-out fee is a flat $125. It covers everything you have on site, and if you move forward with the repair, that $125 comes off your total labor cost.

Electrical Work Done Right

Electrical issues are where a lot of shops struggle. A failing alternator, a worn starter, a corroded ground or a chewed-up wiring harness can all leave you stranded. We trace the fault to the source and repair it properly so the problem does not come back a week later.

Common Signs You Need a Diagnostic

  • Check engine, battery or ABS light is on
  • Engine cranks but will not start, or will not crank at all
  • Dim or flickering lights and electrical accessories
  • Clicking sound when you turn the key
  • Battery keeps dying overnight

If any of that sounds familiar, send us a text with your location and what you are seeing. Photos of the dash and any warning lights help us come prepared.
