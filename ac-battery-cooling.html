A/C, Battery & Cooling System Repair

Florida does not go easy on your vehicle. Heat drains batteries, strains A/C systems and pushes cooling systems to the limit. We handle all three on site so you are not left sweating on the side of the road.

Text About This Service See Pricing

Built for Florida Heat

The same sun that makes Central Florida great is brutal on cars. High temperatures shorten battery life, wear out A/C compressors and put constant stress on your cooling system. We come prepared for exactly these problems, with the tools and common parts to fix them on the spot.

Battery Testing & Replacement

A weak battery can leave you stranded anywhere. We test your battery and charging system on site, and if it needs replacing we can handle it right there. Battery service is $125 plus the cost of the new battery if a replacement is needed.

A/C & Cooling Repair

If your air is blowing warm or your temperature gauge is creeping up, do not push it and risk a bigger failure. We diagnose A/C and cooling problems, from low refrigerant and bad compressors to leaking radiators, hoses and failed thermostats, and get your system back to doing its job.

Warning Signs to Watch For

  • A/C blowing warm or weak air
  • Temperature gauge climbing into the red
  • Sweet smell or puddles of coolant under the vehicle
  • Slow crank or clicking when starting
  • Battery or check engine light on
